04 / Process

Five stages. No mystery months.

The worst part of a renovation is not the dust. It is not knowing what happens next.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    We walk the space, listen to what is not working, and talk honestly about budget before anyone draws anything. Free, about an hour.

    You leave with A realistic budget range
  2. 02

    Design and selections

    Layouts, materials, and fixtures chosen together, with samples in hand and in your light rather than from a catalogue.

    You leave with A fixed scope and a firm number
  3. 03

    Permitting

    We pull permits and handle the county. In Lee and Collier this is where most projects stall, so we build the wait into your schedule.

    You leave with Approved permits and a real start date
  4. 04

    Build

    One crew, sequenced trades, and a site broomed at the end of every day. You get a weekly update from an owner, including the weeks when the news is a delay.

    You leave with Weekly updates, no surprise change orders
  5. 05

    Punch and walkthrough

    We walk it with you and write down everything you notice, not just what we notice. The list closes out before we ask for final payment.

    You leave with A closed punch list and warranty in writing
The honest part

Where projects actually go wrong.

Permitting

County review times move. We quote a range, not a promise, and update you the day it changes.

Selections

Late material choices stall framing. We lock finishes before demo starts for a reason.

Hidden conditions

Older SWFL homes hide rot and out-of-code wiring. We price it the day we find it, in writing.
